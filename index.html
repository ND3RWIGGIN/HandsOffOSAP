The Virtual
Student Network

The Virtual Student Network is an online space created to facilitate students in expressing their concerns towards sociopolitical issues. Before the creation of the VSN, there were no easy access hubs for students to connect and share their viewpoints regarding the decisions directly affecting them. Now, students across Ontario have a reliable way to communicate, stay informed on the latest news, and browse archived political notions they may have missed. The VSN provides all students an opportunity to represent themselves and protect their future.

About

Who We Are
Our student hub serves as a way for students to voice their concerns about social issues through our digital platform. We use our social network as a platform for students to connect, be informed, and tackle current student issues.
Our Mission
Empower Ontario students to voice their concerns and take action on issues that affect their education and future.
What We Do
We provide a platform for students to connect, share stories, stay informed on news, and advocate for change together.
Who We Serve
Students across Ontario — from high school to post-secondary — who want to make their voices heard.

The Ontario government announced $6.4 billion in funding for universities and colleges to support long-term post-secondary growth and sustainability. Schools will also be permitted to raise tuition fees by up to 2% annually after a lengthy freeze.
